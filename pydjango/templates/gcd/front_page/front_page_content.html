We're a nonprofit, Internet-based organization of international volunteers dedicated to building a database covering all printed comics throughout the world, and we're glad you're here! Give our search form a try, or take a look at the menu to the left to see how you can help us improve the site.

New Features for September 2012!

  • Scans of the indicia (and corresponding legal-like printed publication data) or the statement of ownership can now be added to our site. Look for the 'Resources' link on the right of an issue page.
  • First step in our restructuring of brands: To identify brands an image can be uploaded. More changes to brands will come in the next two months.

New Features for April 2012!

Major changes
  • We now use reprint links instead of reprint notes. A reprint link connects two sequences, a sequence and an issue, or two issues, respectively. It can be edited or added from both sides, and then displays the corresponding reprint information at both places. More information and screencasts can be found under reprints in our documentation wiki.
  • We added keywords.
  • Comic sequences in non-comic publications can be added to our database.
Other changes visible to all
  • Issue shows as indexed if more than 40% of total pages are indexed.
  • Added partial indexed status: at least one comic story is indexed.
  • Variants are not used in issue navigation.
  • Number of variants shows as *number in issue navigation dropdown.
  • Show on-sale date and barcode on series detail/timeline pages.
  • Several bug fixes.
Changes visible for contributors
  • One can remember a story, a cover, or an issue. This is particularly useful for reprint link editing. See the remember-link in the top right of a sequence box.
  • Issue import/export can be done in csv-format in addition to tab-separated.
  • Changeset comments are emailed to all who commented before.
